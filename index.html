    Reference & Notes

    Quick Convert — supported units
    • Length: metres, kilometres, miles, feet, inches, nautical miles, light-years
    • Weight / Mass: grams, kilograms, pounds, ounces, tonnes, stone
    • Volume: litres, millilitres, US gallons, US fluid oz, cups, tablespoons, teaspoons
    • Area: m², km², ft², in², acres, hectares
    • Speed: m/s, km/h, mph, knots, ft/s
    • Temperature: °C, °F, Kelvin
    • Data: bytes, KB, MB, GB, TB, PB (IEC binary)
    • Fuel Economy: L/100km, mpg (US), mpg (UK), km/L
    • Angle: degrees, radians, gradians, arcminutes, arcseconds
    • Currency: Live rates via Frankfurter.app (AUD base)
    Engineering Wizard — coverage
    • Pressure: Pa, kPa, MPa, bar, psi, atm
    • Torque: N·m, lb·ft, kg·cm, in·lbf
    • Power: W, kW, MW, hp (mech), BTU/hr
    • Energy: J, kJ, cal, kcal, Wh, kWh, BTU
    • Stress & Safety Factor: mix N/lbf/kN with mm²/in²/cm²; yield in MPa/psi
    • Drill & Tap: ISO metric M3–M12, UNC & UNF presets + custom formula
    • Thread: pitch ↔ TPI, lead, helix angle for any start count

    All calculations run in-browser. Verify critical engineering values against applicable standards and manufacturer data before use.
